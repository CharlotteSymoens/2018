It's a short story that I could easily spell out in 2 sentences.

But I want to go deeper into the unspeakable matter of the story.

Let me be pretentious or let's call it ambitious if I tell you that my goal is to 

enter the mystical realm of this story. Explore the invisible layers and 

make them visible in a concrete form. But without touching the mysticism of it.




Purpose:

To begin with: rethink purpose. Why are we doing things in general?

Is it an outer force that drives us, is it you (the spectator) that drives us?

We want to reinvent ourselves, theatre, art, life and

at our top level we want to reinvent you, the spectator.

Maybe the purpose will be to erase all purpose.


Meaning:

Everything you think it means, you should think twice, or just stop thinking at move along 

in your imagination. Let go of your preconsumption. In the best case I would love 

to get you in a trance and make you stop judging, feeling, comparing, ...

I want to make you watch and experience.

Breath in and out and get absorped into the story.
