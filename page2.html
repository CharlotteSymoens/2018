Why the first line is the most important one. 



Content:

It can be an audition.

Is it really about something?

Is this something so specific or is it about more than one thing.

I could spill you a content, but will you be satisfied?

Will we understand what I'm writing about? Is it worth our time?

Or am I being too shy or afraid the content isn't worth talking about?

And by saying that am I relativating myself to prepare you for the worst?

The content could be a question.

Longing for no particular answers.

I could choose whatever subject that comes to my mind. Unconsciously driven by my

own desires, but will it touch you as well? Will it be relevant for you? Or will you be bored or

maybe you feel left out, because you never felt what I'm talking about.

Every story can be summarised in some sentences, like the backside of a book.

Like the trailer of a movie, uitgewrongen tot alleen het omhulsel overblijft.



It's a short story that I could easily spell out in 2 sentences.

But I want to go deeper into the unspeakable matter of the story.

Let me be pretentious or let's call it ambitious if I tell you that my goal is to 

enter the mystical realm of this story. Explore the invisible layers and 

make them visible in a concrete form. But without touching the mysticism of it.




Purpose:

I want to walk the lines between fiction and reality. Are we all acting or are we reacting?

What are we doing? What's behind my lines, my walk.

I want to make you believe in something.

I WANT YOU TO BELIEVE ME.

I WANT YOU TO BELIEVE.

To begin with: rethink purpose. Why are we doing things in general?

Is it an outer force that drives us, is it you (the spectator) that drives us?

We want to reinvent ourselves, theatre, art, life and

at our top level we want to reinvent you, the spectator.

Maybe the purpose will be to erase all purpose.


Meaning:

Everything you think it means, you should think twice, or just stop thinking and move along 

in your imagination. Let go of your preassumption. In the best case I would love 

to get you in a trance and make you stop judging, feeling, comparing, ...

I want to make you watch and experience.

Breath in and out and get absorped into the story. 

No Catharsis, no empathy, no apathy,..

Just be
